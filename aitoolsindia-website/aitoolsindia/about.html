Who We Are

AIToolsIndia is an independent blog dedicated to reviewing and comparing AI tools for Indian users. We focus on what actually matters for our audience — real INR pricing, Indian payment method compatibility, Hindi/regional language support, and use cases relevant to Indian students, freelancers, and entrepreneurs.

We test every tool we review. No paid placements. No sponsored reviews. Our ratings are based on real usage.

Affiliate Disclosure

AIToolsIndia participates in affiliate programs. This means we earn a commission when you click our links and make a purchase — at no additional cost to you.

Our affiliate relationships include but are not limited to:

  • Jasper AI Affiliate Program
  • SEMrush Affiliate Program (BeRush)
  • Writesonic Affiliate Program
  • Amazon Associates (India)
  • Canva Affiliate Program
  • ShareASale and Impact.com networks

Our affiliate relationships do NOT influence our reviews or ratings. We only recommend tools we have personally tested and believe provide genuine value. If we don't like a tool, we say so — even if it pays a high commission.
